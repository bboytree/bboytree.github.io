Bala’s Nature Scrapbook

I like to experience nature with my head in the clouds and my hands in my pockets.

Over time, I also wanted to learn about the interesting things I had seen that day.
So I started taking photos as a note to research once I got home.
Or just the cool things that caught my eye.

I had tons of fun living and capturing these moments.
I think the trees, plants, flowers, and mushrooms did too.
